About Janice

More Than a Transaction — A Partnership Built on Trust

Hi, I'm Janice Divina Liad — REALTOR® with HomeSmart PV & Associates, based in Manteca. I specialize in serving multicultural families, first-time buyers, and Bay Area transplants who are building their future in San Joaquin County.

My approach is rooted in the Ninja Selling philosophy — which means I lead with connection, not commission. Before we ever look at a single home, I take time to truly understand your family's goals, your timeline, and what "home" really means to you.

Whether you're buying your first home, selling to upsize, or making the Bay Area move — I bring warmth, market expertise, and a genuine commitment to your family's success at every step.

The Ninja Buyer Process

How I Help You Find the Right Home Without Paying Too Much

My proven 10-step buyer process is built around your goals — not my commission.

1

Greeting & Connection

We meet, connect, and I learn your story — your family, your goals, your timeline.

2

Prior Learning Interview

I ask the right questions to understand your past experiences and what you want to change.

3

Buyer Packet & Agency Disclosure

Full transparency — I walk you through every document so you know exactly what you're signing.

4

The Funnel Process

We use systematic elimination, not random selection. Don't miss anything. Don't pay too much.

5

Scale of 1 to 10

We identify where you are in your journey and what it takes to get you to a confident "10."

6

Pieces of Paper Exercise

You define your top 3 must-haves and knockout factors — so we always stay focused.

7

Features & Benefits (The 85% Home)

The perfect home rarely exists — but the right home does.

8

Financing Strategy

Cash, loan, house to sell? I walk you through the 1% = 10% rule and connect you with trusted lenders.

9

Offer & Negotiation

When we find the one — we move fast and smart.

10

Close & Celebrate

Keys in hand, welcome home. You're family now.
