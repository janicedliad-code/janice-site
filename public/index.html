About Janice

More Than a Transaction — A Partnership Built on Trust

Hi, I'm Janice Divina Liad — REALTOR® with HomeSmart PV & Associates, based in Manteca. I specialize in serving multicultural families, first-time buyers, and Bay Area transplants building their future in San Joaquin County.

My approach is rooted in a relationship-based philosophy — which means I lead with connection, not commission. Before we ever look at a single home, I take time to truly understand your family's goals, your timeline, and what "home" really means to you.

Whether you're buying your first home, selling to upsize, or making the Bay Area move — I bring warmth, market expertise, and a genuine commitment to your family's success at every step.
